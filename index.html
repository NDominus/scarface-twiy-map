Scarface TWIY Map

Utility for keeping progress in the videogame Scarface: The World Is Yours
By NDominus

Instructions

This is a utility for keeping track of which gangs have been defeated in the game. You can mark them as defeated by clicking on the skull icons in the map. You can switch to any of the 4 zones of the game (Little Havana, Downtown, South Beach and North Beach) by selecting them in the top navigation bar.

You can save your progress of each zone by copying the password generated in the input field above the interactive map. You can also load your progress by pasting the password in the input field and clicking the "Submit Password" button. Note that a different password is used for each zone of the game.
